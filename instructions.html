Instructions

  1. Drag and drop the topmost disc to any tower.
  2. Rearrange the discs to ultimately stack them in the third tower according to the original order.

Rules

  • Only one disc from a tower can be moved each time.
  • Each move consists of taking the topmost disc from one of the towers and placing it on top of another tower.
  • A bigger disc cannot be placed on top of a smaller disc.
