Instructions

Move all rings to pile C and stack them according to the original order. Click and drag a ring to the target pile, then click again to place it.

Rules

  1. Only one ring on a pile can be moved each time.
  2. Each move consists of taking the topmost ring from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack.
  3. A bigger ring cannot be placed on top of a smaller ring.
